# Optimized Job Description

**1. Job Title & Location**
Senior Data Engineer
London, UK (Hybrid)

**2. About the Company**
GreenPay is a rapidly growing FinTech startup revolutionizing the financial landscape with innovative, sustainable payment solutions. We are building a platform that empowers users and businesses to make smarter, greener financial choices. Driven by a mission to blend cutting-edge technology with environmental responsibility, GreenPay is at the forefront of the sustainable finance movement, offering secure, efficient, and impactful payment experiences. Join a dynamic team committed to creating a positive global impact through financial innovation.

**3. Role Overview**
As a Senior Data Engineer at GreenPay, you will play a pivotal role in architecting, building, and maintaining our robust and scalable data infrastructure. You will be responsible for designing and implementing advanced ETL/ELT pipelines, managing our data warehouse/lake, and ensuring the highest standards of data quality, security, and accessibility. Your work will directly enable data-driven decision-making across the company, from product development and risk management to financial reporting and strategic planning, supporting GreenPay's rapid growth and mission to innovate sustainable finance.

**4. Key Responsibilities**
*   Design, develop, and maintain highly scalable, reliable, and efficient ETL/ELT pipelines for batch and real-time data processing.
*   Architect and optimize our data warehouse/data lake solutions, ensuring data integrity, security, and accessibility.
*   Collaborate closely with Data Scientists, Analysts, and Product teams to understand data requirements and deliver impactful data solutions.
*   Implement robust data quality checks, monitoring, and governance frameworks to ensure accuracy and reliability.
*   Optimize data models and queries for performance and scalability, handling large volumes of financial data.
*   Evaluate and integrate new data technologies and tools to enhance our data platform capabilities.
*   Ensure compliance with data privacy regulations and industry best practices for data security in a FinTech context.
*   Mentor and provide technical guidance to more junior data engineers, fostering a culture of excellence.
*   Troubleshoot and resolve complex data-related issues and ensure operational stability of data systems.

**5. Required Skills & Experience**
*   5+ years of professional experience in Data Engineering, preferably within a fast-paced environment.
*   Strong proficiency in SQL and at least one programming language (Python highly preferred).
*   Extensive experience with cloud-based data platforms (e.g., AWS, GCP, or Azure, with AWS preferred).
*   Proven track record with modern data warehousing solutions (e.g., Snowflake, Redshift, Google BigQuery, Databricks).
*   Expertise in designing, building, and optimizing robust ETL/ELT pipelines.
*   Solid understanding of data modeling techniques (dimensional, Kimball) and schema design.
*   Experience with data orchestration tools (e.g., Apache Airflow, Prefect, Dagster).
*   Familiarity with version control systems (Git) and CI/CD practices.
*   Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.

**6. Nice-to-Have Skills**
*   Prior experience in the FinTech, payments, or banking industry.
*   Experience with real-time data streaming technologies (e.g., Kafka, Kinesis).
*   Knowledge of specific AWS services (e.g., Glue, Lambda, Kinesis, S3, RDS).
*   Familiarity with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
*   Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker, Power BI).
*   Understanding of data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) and security best practices relevant to financial data.
*   Contributions to open-source projects or a strong GitHub profile.
